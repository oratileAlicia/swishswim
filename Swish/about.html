SWISH Swimming School

SWISH Swimming School, established in 2019, is a premier swimming school located conveniently on Bergrivier Drive, in Kempton Park. Founded on a passion for teaching children how to swim and be safe around water has grown to now offer comprehensive swimming lessons for all age groups. With our certified professional instructors, a large indoor heated pool and clean facilities, Swish is committed to providing the best swimming experience for our swimmers.

MISSION

At Swish Swimming School, our mission is to equip children and adults with the necessary skills and confidence to become competent swimmers. We understand the importance of swimming as a life skill, especially in South Africa's water-centric lifestyle. Our aim is to prioritize water safety and instill a love for swimming while teaching valuable skills beyond the ability to swim unaided.

Benefits of Joining

  • Water Safety: Strong focus on teaching water safety.
  • Qualified and SSA Certified Instructors: Experienced and Swim South Africa certified instructors.
  • Large Indoor Heated Pool: Indoor heated pool for year-round comfort.
  • Clean Facilities: Maintained to high standards of cleanliness.
  • All Age Groups: Lessons available for children, teenagers, and adults.
  • Low Student to Instructor Ratio: Low student-to-instructor ratio (max 4:1) for personalized attention.
